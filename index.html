What we do

Why are so many businesses moving to the cloud? It is because cloud computing increases efficiency, helps improve cash flow, is capital expenditure free, improves collaboration, empowers mobility, and is environmentally friendly. Our core services are designed to help your business realise the potential of cloud computing. From consulting through to managed services, HUGMYPC can help you develop and impliment a cost-effective cloud solution that works for you, rather than you working for IT!

Managed Services

There's no need for dedicated resources in-house. Our team have the knowledge and expertise to support your business operations round the clock. We make sure your cloud services are managed and proactively maintained, take care of backups, system updates, and minimise overheads to your IT so that you can focus on what's really important, your business.

Consulting Services

Our consulting and professional services can add value to your business by providing strategy, architecture, implementation and integration services to help plan, build, improve and innovate your IT and business solutions. We provide these services across a range of technologies including cloud computing, mobility, end-user computing, communications?, and security.

Cloud App Development

From design to execution, we engage with our clients to understand their needs and the behaviours of their target audience to deliver a world class user experience. We work with individuals, companies, and entrepreneurs to execute ideas into successful concepts. Our team is leading across a wide spectrum of design, technical analysis, programming and online marketing.

Activity Based Working

ABW is a concept to design your office in such a way that it supports work activities optimally, rather than drawing a boundary around a users' personal spaces, i.e. when everyone sits at a uniform desk with all their gear in one place. Users can concentrate better, be more productive, attract and retain talent, be more active and mindful, and enjoy their work more.
